<h2>About the Project</h2>
<h3>Background</h3>
<p>The Unreel Take is a curated library of reviews for a large variety of media such as movies, books, and TV shows. Authors aim to provide readers with their own personal experience of a particular title, rather than just assigning it an arbitrary, often opinionated, rating.
</p>
<p>The Unreel team is a group of like-minded friends who are passionate authors, filmmakers and moviegoers that enjoy sharing their experiences. Their hope is that anyone who visits the website will share in their passion and is able to find a review that they can connect with.</p>
<h3>The Problem</h3>
<p>The Product Owner needs a blog posting website that supports a publicly viewable frontend and a private, invitation-only, backend.</p>
<h3>The Solution</h3>
<h4>Frontend</h4>
<p>Our frontend solution implements a public home route complete with a searchable catalog, an about page, and media request form.</p>
<h4>Backend</h4>
<p>Our backend solution implements a private dashboard that supports user roles, invitation and account setup, a rich text editor for publishing and editing content, and a suite of other tools for general website management.</p>
